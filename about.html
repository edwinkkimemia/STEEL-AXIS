Who We Are

Steel Axis Engineering Ltd, founded in 2005 and headquartered in Manchester, UK, is a multi-disciplinary engineering firm specializing in mechanical and electrical installations, piping work, steel fabrication, and health and safety consultancy. Our commitment to excellence and innovation has made us a trusted partner for industrial and manufacturing clients across the globe.

Our History

From our founding in 2005 to becoming a trusted name in engineering, explore our journey.

2010

2015

2020
