Who We Are

Steel Axis Engineering Ltd., established in 2017 and headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya, is a dynamic, multi-disciplinary engineering company serving industrial clients across East and Central Africa. We specialize in mechanical engineering, electrical solutions, industrial automation, power systems, turnkey projects, feed processing machines, and personal protective equipment (PPE). Our comprehensive services include feasibility studies, design, installation, commissioning, and maintenance, catering to sectors like manufacturing, food processing, construction, and energy. We operate under ISO 3834 Part 2 standards, prioritizing safety, quality, and sustainability in every project.
